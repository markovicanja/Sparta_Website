ALL ABOUT SPARTA
Sparta is a unique gym and fitness center in Belgrade. As one of the larger gyms, it has 1,500 square feet. It is a superbly equipped space where you can do complete fitness training whether you are a recreational athlete or an athlete of any sport.
It is necessary to mention that Sparta always invests in new exercise equipment so its members have access to showers and large locker rooms.
You are also given the option of having your own membership card with which you are allowed one entry per day, having unlimited stay at the gym.
Sparta is specific because it offers a number of group classes in addition to the gym programs that can be used together and combined as desired.
GOALS
OUR MISSION
Sparta is designed to provide workout conditions for all the most demanding athletes as well as recreational athletes and children. Hundreds of different machines of the last generation offer athletes such training conditions which can not be found anywhere else in region.
Sparta exists out of love and commitment to sport and the idea of ​​Sparta is the popularization and stimulation of people, youth and children to play sports. Our goal is to help everyone who needs help and who we are able to help.
