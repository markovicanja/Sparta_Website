Nutritionist
A nutritionist is there to help you stay healthy and to safely reach your desired goals and transform your body. The Internet is full of mostly harmful and inefficient diets which lead to a jo-jo effect, and at worst, a deterioration of your health.
How does a nutritionist get the information they need to provide the best possible treatment?
The sessions last from 30 to 45 minutes. During this period the nutritionist collects data from scales, then gives you a questionnaire and then you together go through your daily routines (eg sitting at work, eating out commonly etc.). After this assessment, the nutritionist creates right diet plan that you can get in three days at the reception of our gym.
Creating a personalized diet plan
Each person who turns to a nutritionist for help will receive a special treatment, and a diet plan that fits their fitness goals, and physical health. People with health problems like diabetes or high blood pressure must take particular care about nutrition, and the nutritionist designs individually for each person an adequate diet plan that, combined with training, lead to the desired transformation of the body and above all, healthy and a happier life.
After all, inadequate nutrition rich in trans fatty acids and sugars can cause a number of health problems.
