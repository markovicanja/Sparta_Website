Ashtanga Yoga

Workout description
Ashtanga yoga is a style based on the exercises of Ashtanga and Vinjasa yoga. By connecting breath and movement it leads to better flexibility and strength while enhancing concentration. It calms the mind and eliminates stress.

Ashtanga yoga functions as a continuous movement: a transition from one yoga pose to another without a break. That leads to intense warming and sweating through which the body is naturally purified.
