Massages

Sports massage
Sports massage is the type of massage that is recommended to both professional athletes and recreational athletes whose the body is subjected to greater exertion.
The goal of sports massages is to eliminate all the harmful and unintended consequences that are a result from it psychophysical effort, as quickly as possible.
Therapeutic massage
Therapeutic massage is a set of manual techniques that are used to keep the musculature relaxed and to make it easier for the oxygen and nutrients to get to tired muscle. It aims to produce certain therapeutic effects and benefits. Therapeutic massage is usually moderate intensity.
Anti-cellulite massage
Anti-cellulite massage is perfect, effective, completely harmless and healthy way to remove cellulite, because it has multiple effects.
By regular massaging fat cells are broken, circulation is increased and toxins are released as well as excess water in the body.
Maderotherapy
It is a massage technique that comes from Colombia. It is based on ancient oriental manipulation technique a tree whose focus is on eliminating cellulite. Represents a deeper form of massage that helps to improve balance energy, stress disappears and muscles and joints are released from pain.
