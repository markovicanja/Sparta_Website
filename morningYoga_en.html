Morning Yoga

Workout description
Morning yoga is the best way to start a new day with a smile on your face. Through vinjasa flow, you will grow, empower and heal your body. You will connect breath and movement and strike a balance between mind and body.

Everyone who wants to increase their energy levels is welcome. This workout will bring harmony to your life, strengthen your body and you will learn how to resist stress lifestyle. Once you feel the benefits of morning practice, you will never get rid of this healthy habit.
